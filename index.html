How to Play:

• Avoid red obstacles on the ground

• Collect GOLD artifacts for special powers

• Tap power-up icons to activate them

• Solve 3 historical puzzles to progress

Power-ups:

🛡️ Shield - Tap to become invincible for 10 seconds

⏱️ Time Freeze - Tap to stop all obstacles for 8 seconds
